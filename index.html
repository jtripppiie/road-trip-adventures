      Hide & Seek Adventure

      A local pass-and-play room search game. The hider moves room to room, commits to real cover, then hands the phone over while the seeker burns a limited number of searches.

      Pass & Play
      Setup

      Pick a map, difficulty, and match length. Hiders get a minute to settle into cover. Seekers only get a handful of real checks, so room choices matter.

      Ready

      Start the match.

      Use arrow keys, WASD, or the pad. Sprint drains stamina. Hiders need to commit to cover, seekers inspect from close range, and every bad search burns the round.

      Ready? Start the match, then pass the phone when the app says to.

        About Road Trip Adventures
        
      
        Road Trip Adventures was built to help everyone enjoy the ride together. It offers fun challenges that encourage looking outside, laughing together, learning new facts, and gentle competition. There are no accounts, logins or data collection. All progress and preferences stay on your device and disappear when you close the page.
        
      
        Family‑Friendly Content
        
      
        All activities are designed to be appropriate for families.  We avoid profanity, adult themes, political or religious persuasion, insults and dangerous challenges.  If you ever encounter content that doesn’t feel right, please close the adventure and let the creator know.
        
      
        Privacy
        
      
        We respect your privacy. This site does not collect personal information, use location sensors, track your trip, or send data to any server. Local trivia is based only on the region you choose manually. There is no GPS, no browser location prompt, and no location data to save or upload.
